AlexNet to Sort Cars From Semi-Trucks (In Progress)

Click Here To Try a Demo of the Code

This project involved training the AlexNet model on a dataset of images of cars and semi trucks, and then uses the model to automatically sort incoming vehicles. This could greatly increase the efficiency charging highway tolls. The economic importance of this project lies in the potential cost savings and increased productivity that can be achieved through toll automation. Additionally, this technology could be scaled up and applied to other areas of logistics, further increasing its economic impact.

AlexNet to Identify Flowers (In Progress)

Click Here To Try a Demo of the Code

This project involved training the AlexNet model on a dataset of images of flowers. The goal is too be able to input an image of a flower and have it species be identified. An AlexNet model trained to sort flowers could be used to help automate the process of identifying and sorting flowers in a greenhouse or a flower shop. This could potentially save time and reduce errors in the sorting process, as well as provide more accurate information about the different types of flowers available.
